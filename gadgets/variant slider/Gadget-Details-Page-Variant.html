About BlackBerry Z3

The Blackberry Z3 features a 5.0-inch display with a resolution of 540 x 960 pixels and is powered by a 1.2GHz dual core qualcomm MSM8230 processor and has 1.5GB of RAM. It comes with 8GB of inbuilt storage, expandable up to 32GB via microSD card and has a 5.0-megapixel rear camera and 1.1-megapixel front camera. It comes with BlackBerry 10 OS and support a 2500mAh battery capacity with 3G.
